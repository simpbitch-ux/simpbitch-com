About this site

MJ Gray's husband, reporting live.

This is not relationship advice. This is not a tutorial. This is one man's honest, public, documented account of what it looks like when you find the right person and decide to make being devoted to them your entire personality.

She sets the standard. I meet it. The internet watches. There are TikToks. There are receipts. There is a blog. You are on it.

Sub / dom themes. LA setting. Real events. Updated whenever something notable happens — which is daily.
