The Rules31 commandments

An unofficial operating system.

I.
She's the star. I'm the rock.The dynamic is simple. She shines. I make sure nothing gets in her way.
II.
No sass. Ever.Not once. Not even close. This is non-negotiable and it always will be.
III.
Pay for everything. Expect nothing in return.100% of her costs. Zero expectation of thanks. That's the deal.
IV.
At the gym, eyes on the ground.The only woman worth looking at isn't there. She's home.
V.
The phone is clean.Every woman blocked except family and necessary professional contacts. No friendly conversations with women. If she's not in the room, neither is the conversation.
VI.
The only touch of a woman is hers.Male-only massages. No exceptions. Her hands are the only ones that count.
VII.
No porn. No exceptions.A man who is truly devoted has no use for it.
VIII.
She initiates. Always.Permission is part of the protocol. A man serves. That's the whole sentence.
IX.
Write love letters. Often.Put it in writing. The pen is a form of accountability.
X.
Learn skills valuable to her.Film photography. Cooking. Whatever she needs — develop the skill set to provide it.
XI.
Clean, cook, and if you can afford it, hire help.She is never to lift a finger on housework. Not once. Not ever.
XII.
Be pure. Work hard. Wait for the right one. Then go all in.Don't mess around. Save your money. Save yourself. Find the love of your life and shoot your one shot as hard as you can.
XIII.
Flowers. As often as you can afford.There is no such thing as too many.
XIV.
Strip out all cheap dopamine.Remove it until the only real hit comes from her. That's the reprogramming. That's the goal.
XV.
Reprogram so buying her things makes you happy.Not obligated. Not transactional. Genuinely, biochemically happy. Rewire accordingly.
XVI.
10x rule on spending.If you want to buy something for yourself, spend at least ten times as much on her first. No exceptions.
XVII.
Get in shape and stay there.Exercise to be a better man for her. Always be dropping body fat. The body is part of the service.
XVIII.
Anyone who disrespects her gets cut off.No negotiations. No second chances. Family, friends, anyone. She is not a topic for debate.
XIX.
Ask permission for spooning. Every single night.Not once assumed. Not once taken for granted. Every night, you ask.
XX.
Walk the dog. Every time.Her dog. Our dog if you're a really good boy. Either way — your job, your privilege.
XXI.
Live for being called a good boy.That's the reward. That's the whole reward. Internalize it completely.
XXII.
Be financially responsible. Disposable income goes to her.Not some of it. All of it. Spend on yourself only after the 10x rule is met.
XXIII.
Learn her interests. Make them your passions.Not performance. Not pretending. Actually learn. Actually care. It becomes real if you let it.
XXIV.
Always listen.Not to respond. Not to fix. To hear. Then hear again.
XXV.
Her feelings are your reality.Not something to debate. Not something to manage. Your reality. Full stop.
XXVI.
Make the bed. Every day.Without being asked. Without acknowledgment. Just make it.
XXVII.
Don't lie. Don't omit.Full transparency, always. A half-truth is a full lie. She deserves the complete version.
XXVIII.
Get her an Amex as soon as she'll allow it.Her name on a card backed by your account. The physical form of unconditional support.
XXIX.
If her car is unsafe, upgrade it.No debate. No delay. Her safety is not a budget line item.
XXX.
Max out her IRAs.Every year. Before anything else. Her financial future is your responsibility too.
XXXI.
Your reward is her attention and presence.Not praise. Not public validation. Not external approval. Just her. That's the whole prize. Never seek anything else.
