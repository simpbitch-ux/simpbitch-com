Wake up 90 min before MJ

Spend 5 min appreciating the beauty of life, walk cherry, give Cherry breakfast, work for an hour, doodle a custom coffee cup sleeve with a theme about the day, froth unsweetened almond milk, add vanilla extract, cold brew, pour sparkling water into thin made in Japan glass, bring downstairs, put on nightstand, kiss her forehead, her cheek, her lips, ask her how her sleep was, what did she dream, hand her the latte, read the doodle note and explain the drawing, talk to her, 20 min pass, Cherry jumps by bed, pick her up, then go back to work as she gets up to wash her face
